Gaur Legacy At Jaypee Greens, Greater Noida

Gaur Legacy, located within the prestigious Jaypee Greens in Greater Noida, offers a unique blend of luxury and tranquility. These ultra-luxurious 4 BHK apartments, available in expansive 3600 and 4800 sq. ft. configurations, overlook a breathtaking 18-hole golf course and are designed for those who seek an exclusive and sophisticated lifestyle. With pre-launch pricing starting at ₹15,500 per sq ft, residents can enjoy the benefits of a limited-time offer and a convenient 25X4 payment plan. Key amenities include a 50,000 sq ft clubhouse, sports complex, resort, and world-class facilities. With only 250 units available, Gaur Legacy promises privacy and high-end living in one of Greater Noida’s most desirable locations.

About Builder

Founded in 1995, Gaursons India Limited, led by Mr. Manoj Gaur and family, is a prominent real estate company based in Ghaziabad. With a focus on customer satisfaction and technological integration, they have successfully delivered over 5.5 million sq. ft. of projects in Delhi NCR. The company has completed around 65 commercial and residential developments, primarily in Noida, Ghaziabad, and the Yamuna Expressway. Gaursons also excels in hospitality, education, contracting, and infrastructure, high-quality solutions.
Gaursons India is committed to building futuristic, technologically advanced projects that cater to modern living. With a strong track record of delivering top-quality developments, the company continues to shape the real estate landscape in Delhi NCR
